About Me

I graduated from UNR in May 2018 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering. I am a young software developer seeking opportunity to learn and thrive in the professional workplace. I worked on various projects throughout my past 5 years and have had experience in following languages: C, C++, C#, GLSL, Java, Javascript, and HTML. My strongest languages are C# and C++ but I am perfectly capable of learning what is needed to get the job done without the work being sloppy.

I enjoy doing things like hiking, gaming, and playing board games with family and friends. Although I love graphics, my interest is truely in development and being able to see what I have done and see it be used in the world. I love development and problem solving, its what I consider to be the most beneficial about me.
